## Redaction Patterns
 - `redactDigits`: Replace all digits with X's. Do not hide/remove the field upon download or access.
   - Only Apply To: NOT `zip_code`
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Find: Any digit [0-9]
   - Replace with: "X"
 - `hideField`: Completely hide/remove the field from download or access. Therefore, Regex replace is irrelevant.
   - Hide Field: `true`
 - `replaceWithMessage_t`: Replace the entire string with a custom, translated message. Do not hide/remove the field upon download or access.
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Find: *
   - Replace with: Proscribed, translated string.
 - `truncateToFive`: Apply this pattern only to the zip code field. Truncate to first five numbers. Do not hide/remove the field upon download or access.
   - Only Apply To: `zip_code`
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Pattern: Truncate to first five numbers (if first five are numbers)
 - `redactAll`: Replace the entire string with nothing. In other words, delete the string, but do not hide/remove the field upon access or download.
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Find: *
   - Replace with: ""
 - `convertToBoolean`: Convert the contents to a boolean. If the contents are null, empty, false, 'no', or 'n', then convert the contents to 'false.' Otherwise, convert the contents to 'true' upon download or access.
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Pattern: If `null`, `empty`, `false`, "no", or "n", then `false`, else `true`
 - `noRedaction`: Do not redact or change the field at all, prior to downloading.
   - Hide Field: `false`
   - Pattern: No changes.
 - `inherit`: Inherit the most liberal permissions that an affiliated organization has to this case
   - Only Apply To: `inherit`
   - Hide Field: `inherit`
   - Find: `inherit`
   - Replace with: `inherit`
